About Me

I'm a huge baseball fan and although I am a devoted Chicago Cubs fan, I have a goal of attending a game at every MLB ballpark. As of the end of the 2019 season, I have been to thirteen different parks. When I have extra time, I enjoy woodworking and carpentry. I also travel whenever I have the opportunity.
